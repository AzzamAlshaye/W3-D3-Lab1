My Educational Career

Title description, April 28, 2025

I successfully earned my Bachelor's degree in Information Systems from Imam Muhammad ibn Saud Islamic University, where I studied from 2019 to 2024. Throughout my academic journey, I engaged deeply with both theoretical and practical aspects of computing, technology, and information management. The curriculum provided me with a strong foundation in a wide range of technical subjects, allowing me to develop and sharpen my programming and analytical skills. During my time at the university, I acquired proficiency in several key programming and development tools, including HTML for front-end web development, SQL for data querying and database design, and Java for object-oriented programming and application logic. I also became skilled in Dart and Flutter, which I used to build cross-platform mobile applications with responsive and user-friendly interfaces. Additionally, I worked extensively with Firebase, enhancing my knowledge in real-time databases, authentication services, and cloud-based app functionality. Beyond programming languages and frameworks, I gained valuable hands-on experience working with Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems, understanding their role in integrating various business processes and enhancing organizational efficiency. I explored the Oracle Database platform in depth, learning how to manage data securely, execute complex queries, and optimize system performance.
